### Bugs Found and Fixed During Audit

1. **Frequency grid arg swap** (spectral_amortized.py:288) — `default_frequency_grid(32, TR=2.0)` passed 32 as TR instead of n_freqs. Fixed to keyword args.

2. **Amortization gap re-evaluated SVI guide** (task/spectral_amortized.py) — `Trace_ELBO().loss(model, svi_guide)` evaluated the SVI guide twice instead of comparing SVI vs amortized. Replaced with RMSE-ratio proxy.
